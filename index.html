ARL13B

Ciliary Gene

ADP ribosylation factor like GTPase 13B is a small GTPase that localizes to the ciliary membrane and plays crucial roles in ciliary membrane trafficking and cilia formation.

Gene ID: 200894
Location: Chr 17q21.31
Protein: ARL13B_HUMAN
CilioGenics Score: 0.89

Gene Function

ARL13B encodes a small GTPase belonging to the ADP-ribosylation factor-like (ARL) family. This protein is specifically enriched in primary cilia and plays essential roles in:

  • Ciliary membrane trafficking and composition
  • Hedgehog signaling pathway regulation
  • Maintenance of ciliary structure and stability
  • Regulation of ciliary length
