TSPAN6 (Tetraspanin 6)

Tetraspanin 6 is a member of the tetraspanin family. Tetraspanins are cell surface proteins that are known to form complexes with integrins and other transmembrane proteins. TSPAN6 plays a role in immune response, nerve regeneration, and may be involved in cilia function in certain cell types.
